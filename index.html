♟️ Chess at Worlderly

Structured, competitive chess training for every level

At Worlderly, chess is taught using proven coaching methods followed by professional and tournament-level chess trainers.Focused on fundamentals and long-term competitive chess growth.


Students are placed into levels based on skill and experience, not age, after an initial assessment.

Explorers
From Scratch · Pure Beginners
Suitable for:

Children who are completely new to chess.

How Worlderly Teaches:
  • Step-by-step introduction to piece movements and rules
  • Visual board understanding and basic checkmate ideas
  • Guided practice games under close coach supervision
Learning Outcomes:
  • Clear understanding of how chess works
  • Ability to play a complete game independently
  • Strong foundation for structured chess learning
Builders
Knows piece movement & basic strategies
Suitable for:

Children who understand the basics and are ready to improve structure.

How Worlderly Teaches:
  • Teaching opening principles and proper piece development
  • Introduction to core tactics (forks, pins, skewers)
  • Coach-led analysis of practice games
Learning Outcomes:
  • Improved piece coordination and planning
  • Better in-game decision-making
  • Confidence to play online rated games
Strategists
Rating ~500–900
Suitable for:

Students with competitive exposure looking to improve consistency and performance.

How Worlderly Teaches:
  • Tactical calculation and pattern recognition training
  • Middle-game planning and endgame fundamentals
  • Structured game analysis using online and FIDE-style formats
Learning Outcomes:
  • Consistent rating improvement
  • Stronger positional understanding
  • Improved tournament confidence and performance

Advanced players · Tournament winners
Suitable for:

How Worlderly Teaches:

Learning Outcomes:

  • Long-term progression toward elite chess pathways

Worlderly is an online World School for children, offering live 1:1 and small-group classes in Math, Science, Chess, and STEM.

We focus on joyful, personalised learning, guided with care by talented women teachers.

We currently teach Primary 1 to Primary 6 (P1–P6) students, aligned to the Singapore curriculum and global learning standards.

All classes are LIVE and interactive.

We do not rely on pre-recorded videos, because children learn best through real-time interaction with a teacher.

  • 1:1 classes are fully personalised and move at your child’s pace.
  • Group classes (maximum 4 students) encourage collaboration while still ensuring individual attention.

Both formats follow the same high-quality curriculum and care standards.

Our classes are led by experienced and carefully selected women teachers, including:
